Best Posing Fur Award

In our quarterly contest, we give our winner to a resident squirrel of Utah's Zion National Park. He does not have an official name, so we have been calling him Theo. He was not afraid to run through crowds that were passing, and was definitely not camera shy.

The wonderful photographer of this image is our very own, Ramsey Harris. When coming across the little furball, Ramsey quickly acted by whipping out his phone and snapping a bunch of pictures. He submitted this photo and everyone in the office fell in love with the little guy.

We are still accepting photos for the last quarter of the year. Please submit them with your contact info if you would like the chance to have your photos posted on one of our pages.
