Welcome to the Temple Inn & Suites Homepage

We are an owner-operated company that specializes in providing accomodations, and other needs, for temple workers, patrons, and their families.

With many locations all over, and always located near a temple, you will always find it easy to be close to the temple when traveling.

The temple located near the headquarters of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is a wonderful tourist attraction for all visitors. Dedicated in 1893, it is the sixth temple completed by the church.

It has been announced that the church plans to close this temple on December 29, 2019 for renovations. These renovations are expected to take approximately four years to complete. Please make note of this closure if you have made plans to visit Salt Lake area.
